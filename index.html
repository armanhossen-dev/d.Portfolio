How HealFundry Works

HealFundry is a simple platform designed to help individuals easily and securely transfer money to hospitals for medical expenses. Here's how it works:

  1. Step 1: Sign up or log in to your HealFundry account.
  2. Step 2: Link your bank account to the platform for easy transfers.
  3. Step 3: Select the hospital where your loved one is being treated.
  4. Step 4: Enter the amount to transfer and review the details.
  5. Step 5: Confirm the transfer, and your payment will be securely processed.

Your funds will reach the hospital promptly, ensuring timely medical care for those in need!
